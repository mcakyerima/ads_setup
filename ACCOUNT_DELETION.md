# Account Deletion Request - Kimedata

If you wish to delete your Kimedata account and associated data, please follow these steps:

## How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, send an email to:

**Email:** support@kimedata.com

**Subject:** Account Deletion Request

**Include in your email:**
- Your registered phone number
- Your full name
- Reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request and send you a confirmation email within 48 hours.

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

✅ **Immediately Deleted:**
- Personal information (name, phone number, email)
- Account settings and preferences
- Push notification tokens
- Biometric authentication data
- App usage statistics

✅ **Deleted Within 30 Days:**
- Transaction history (kept for 30 days for accounting purposes)
- Support ticket history

⚠️ **Data We Keep (Legal Requirement):**
- Transaction records for financial audits (kept for 7 years as required by Nigerian law)
- Records are anonymized (your personal details are removed)

## Processing Time

- **Account deactivation:** Immediate
- **Data deletion:** Within 30 days
- **Confirmation:** You'll receive an email within 48 hours confirming your request

## Important Notes

⚠️ Account deletion is **permanent and cannot be undone**

⚠️ Any pending transactions will be completed before deletion

⚠️ Outstanding balances must be resolved before account deletion
